Mac & Cheese

This mac will be goated

Best one to make :)

Ingredients

  • 32oz Chicken Stock
  • 3 1/2 Cups of Elbow Pasta
  • 8 Cups of Cheese of Choice
  • 2 Cups of Whole Milk
  • 1 1/2 Cup of Heavy Cream
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3/4 Cup of Sour Cream
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Drain into another bowl
  3. Add butter to pasta then fully mix
  4. Add Sour Cream to Pasta then Fully Mix
  5. Add seasonings and some cheese to pasta and fully mix
  6. In another bowl mix heavy cream, eggs, seasoning, and whole milk together
  7. Grease up your pan
  8. Layer in past, cheese, sauce in that order
  9. Top with paprika if you'd like
  10. Put in oven for 45min
